How it works

ShareBite connects surplus food donors with verified NGOs. Food can be free or 50% off, and NGOs handle pickup directly.

The full flow

From extra food to completed pickup

01

Donor posts surplus

Mess, hotel, wedding, party hall, or restaurant adds food type, quantity, pickup window, and price option.

02

NGOs reserve pickup

Verified NGOs see nearby listings and reserve only what they can collect and distribute safely.

03

Supplier approves

The supplier checks quantity, pickup time, location, and NGO email before approving the food listing.

04

Direct handover

After approval, an email opens for the NGO. The NGO collects food directly and confirms handover.
